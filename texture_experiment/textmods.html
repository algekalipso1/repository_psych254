In this experiment we will show you sets of three pictures arranged in an equilateral triangle. One of the three pictures will be more different than the other two in some way. Your task will be to identify the "odd one out" in each of the trials. In other words, identify the picture that is different from the other two by clicking on the corresponding button.

You can press the key '1' instead of the 'left' button, the key '2' instead of the 'top' button, and the key '3' instead of the 'right' button, and press the space bar to show the images.

There will be a little 'X' at the center of the equilateral triangle. Stare at that center and only look at the pictures in your peripheral vision.

There will be 4 example trials for you to familiarize yourself with the task, and 66 real trials.

Complete the task in the opened window. When you click subit there you will automatically have submitted to Mechanical Turk. No need to do anything after that.
